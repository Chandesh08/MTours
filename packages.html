Catamaran Cruise

Overview

Take in the highlights of Mauritius on an unforgettable catamaran cruise, with plenty of opportunity to swim, snorkel, and even feed some reef fish. Or, if you prefer, stay on board and enjoy the views while sipping unlimited drinks from the open bar. Madiana sails from Black River Bay to Tamarin Bay, La Preneuse, Le Morne, and Benetier Island, stopping for a savory seafood barbecue lunch paired white and rose wine.

  • An unforgettable catamaran cruise in Mauritius aboard Madiana
  • Plenty of time for swimming, snorkeling, and even feeding reef fish
  • Savor a seafood barbecue lunch with white and rose wine
  • Enjoy unlimited drinks at the open bar

Waterfall

Overview

Combine visits to some of Mauritius' highlights — Seven Coloured Earth, Trou aux Cerfs, and Chamarel Waterfall — during this full-day tour. With a small group limited to 15 people or fewer, taste rum at a local distillery, indulge in a traditional 3-course lunch, and visit the Ship Model Factory in Floreal. Entrance fees and round-trip transportation from Mauritius accommodations are included.

  • Tour of Mauritius’ beautiful southwestern region
  • See Chamarel Waterfall and the Seven Coloured Earth sand dunes
  • Sample rum made at La Rhumerie de Chamarel distillery
  • Savor a 3-course Mauritian lunch at a local restaurant
  • Visit Grand Bassin, a secluded lake and sacred Hindu site
  • Soak up panoramic views from the Trou aux Cerfs volcanic crater

Casela Safari

Overview

Our Safari Pack will bring moments of pure magic and delight to big cat lovers! After a once-in-a-lifetime experience of interacting with lions in their enclosure, you will have the unique opportunity of observing our big cats in the wild and making the most of all activities included in the Entrance ticket. Wonderful memories to cherish forever! The Entrance to the park is included in the package and comprises of the Safari to see zebras, ostriches, white rhinoceros, impalas, antelopes, kudus, wildebeest, 1500 species of birds, Walk-Thru-Aviary, Viewing of giraffe, pygmy hippo, camels and lamas, Petting Farm, Children's playground, Avalanche Mines (Toboggans x2), Big Cats Kingdom, Monkey Kingdom, Tortoise Pen, 4D Cinema & a BRAND NEW RIDE. Available every day except Sundays, valid for persons aged 15 years and above. Closed shoes should be worn. Children should be accompanied by their parents.

  • Tortoises Feeding
  • Lion entrance
  • Big cat drive thru
  • Pony Ride
  • Giraffe & Ostrich Feeding
  • Petting Farm

Dolphins and Volcanic Wonders Tour Including Swimming With Wild Dolphins

Overview

Combine some of Mauritius most important natural landmarks and features in this full-day private tour. Start the morning with snorkeling and swimming in a wild dolphin habitat, then head up to the top of Trou aux Cerfs, a dormant volcano. Hit the crater lake at Grand Bassin, Black River Gorges, and Alexandra Falls, then wind up at the site of the 7-Coloured Earth. Round-trip hotel transport and use of snorkel gear included.

  • Combine the natural wonders of Mauritius in one tour
  • Visit Alexandra Falls, Grand Bassin, and the 7-Coloured Earth
  • Swim and snorkel in a wild dolphin habitat
  • Use of snorkel equipment
  • Round-trip hotel transportation
